Our Mission

The goal of AMEBA is to promote an equitable and inclusive environment that fosters the advancement of PhD students in the Graduate Programs in Bioscience (GPB). Furthermore, we aim to promote diversity in the sciences via outreach to underrepresented communities and youth. AMEBA provides our members with skills and experiences to address these goals.

Stitch & Bitch

Sewing, knitting, and crocheting circles have historically been community building and activist activities. As early as the American Revolution when colonists began spinning their own cloth to boycott British textiles, to abolitionist sewing circles where women gathered to exchange ideas and discuss political activities, up to the present-day--people have found the art of sewing to stimulate intellectual conversation and find community-based solutions to issues of their day!

Date: March 25, 2025
