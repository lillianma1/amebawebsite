Our Mission

The goal of AMEBA is to promote an equitable and inclusive environment that fosters the advancement of PhD students in the Graduate Programs in Bioscience (GPB). Furthermore, we aim to promote diversity in the sciences via outreach to underrepresented communities and youth. AMEBA provides our members with skills and experiences to address these goals.

Upcoming Events

First Friday Coffee Hour

Our monthly coffee hours are held on the first Friday (usually) of each month. This series is designed to provide UCLA graduate researchers with a space to connect, commiserate, and reflect with peers. Select coffee hours are also sponsored by the Behavioral Wellness Center at UCLA and provide mental wellness-oriented activities to provide students and researchers with a toolkit for improving their personal mental wellbeing.

Date: First Friday of Each Month

Pal-entine's Day Social

A community-building event, Pal-entine's Day is an AMEBA-favorite social event to provide UCLA graduate researchers with a space to connect with and bond with peers. This event includes activites for anyone wishing to celebrate friendship, community, and partnership.

Date: February 13th, 2025

"Meet a Scientist Day" Community Outreach Event

In partnership with Heart of Los Angeles (HoLA), this community outreach event invites elementary students to UCLA where they can meet with graduate students and researchers, conduct interactive science experiments, and tour some of the university lab spaces.

Date: March 19th, 2025

Demystifying the Oral Qualifying Exam

An annual event held by AMEBA, this event is designed for first and second year doctoral students to improve transparencey regarding the Oral Qualifying Exam. Students can meet with faculty and graduate students who have advanced to candidacy to understand the expectations, preparation needed for, and outcomes of the Oral Qualifying Exam.

Date: April 2025
